About Us

Tecnicol Moçambique is a professional training center that has existed for 19 years, with the aim of training competent professionals for the labor market and self-employment. We provide short and medium-term professional courses with an average of 800 people (young people and adults) in our delegations in the City of Maputo. Furthermore, we design and implement projects to promote entrepreneurship, recover livelihoods and preparation programs for the labor market for communities in urban and rural areas, in partnership with various public and private sector institutions.
In most of our programs our approach consists of surveying training needs, designing and adapting training curricula, selecting qualified trainers, training trainers, equipping and adapting training sites, identifying and selecting target audiences, training, evaluation of training, monitoring and evaluation to ensure the quality of courses and continuous improvement.

During our long and experienced existence in the market, we act as a strategic partner in the implementation of training projects in the districts of Maputo, Matola, Kamaxaquene, Manhiça, Matutuine, Moatize, Nacala-à-Velha, Nacala Porto, Nampula, Cuamba, Monapo, Inhassoro, Govuro, Vilanculos, Manica, Sofala and Palma-Afungi in various training programs for the following clients:

Labour Market preparation program - LMPP

In this program, 1,000 people were trained in various vocational training courses such as: Industrial Maintenance Mechanics, Industrial Electricity, Welding, Heavy Machinery Operation, Hotel and Restaurant Management, Electricity Installation, Locksmithing, Auto Mechanics, Auto Electricity, Refrigeration.

The objective of this program, which was implemented from September 2021 to October 2023, aims to encourage the hiring of local labor, based on technical & behavioral training, mapping opportunities and creating a database of professionals residing in the areas of interference direct or indirect from the Moatize mine.

At the end of the program, 600 self-employment kits were distributed. This program was implemented in partnership with Vulcan Moçambique.

Training Program in Cutting and Sewing and Women’s Entrepreneurship

One of Tecnicol's first projects in partnership with Vale Moçambique (recently Vulcan Moçambique) implemented in 2014. This project aimed to equip women in the District of Moatize with Cutting and Sewing skills so that they could make clothes and embroider.

In this program, 50 women were trained, who received work kits and created the association of Seamstresses of Moatize, which today is a Cooperative that continues to operate

Believe in Employment Program (Programa Acredita Emprega)

The Believe in Employment program (Programa Acredita Emprega) was implemented in the south of Mozambique where more than 1,500 young people from the districts of KaMaxaquene, Manhiça, Matola and Matutuine were trained.

This program was implemented in partnership with SEJE and the World Bank and aims to improve access to education and employment opportunities for young people. Courses in Carpentry, Cooking, Cutting and Sewing, Refrigeration Systems, Auto Mechanics, Entrepreneurship, Hardware and Plumbing were taught
