Youth Development Programme

Training young people from Inhassoro, Govuro and Vilanculos, with entrepreneurial skills, so that they can start their own small business, as well as supporting those who have some natural entrepreneurial skills, with the ability to manage small businesses, in order to stimulate business growth structured, was the purpose of the Youth Entrepreneurship Promotion Programme implemented in partnership with SASOL.

This program taught Entrepreneurship, Marketing, Basic IT, Business Coaching, Mentoring, Business Incubation and support for participation in national and international business fairs. More than 100 young people were trained.
