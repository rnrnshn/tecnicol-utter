About Us

About Us

Tecnicol Moçambique is a professional training centre with proven track record and 19 years experience, training competent professionals for the labor market and self-employment since 2004.

We offer short and medium-term professional courses to an average of 800 young people and adults in our delegate in the City of Maputo.

Furthermore, we design and implement projects that promote entrepreneurship and recover livelihoods, as well as preparation programs for the labor market for communities in urban and rural areas; in partnership with various public and private sector institutions.

Within most of our programs our approach and process to ensure the quality of courses and continuous improvement consists of Surveying training needs, Designing and adapting training curricula, Selecting qualified trainers, Training trainers, Equipping and adapting training sites, Identifying and selecting target audiences, Training, evaluation and monitoring.

As per performance, we act as a nationwide strategic partner in the implementation of training projects in many districts in Mozambique, including but not limited to Maputo, Matola, Kamaxaquene, Manhiça, Matutuine, Moatize, Nacala-à-Velha, Nacala Porto, Nampula, Cuamba, Monapo, Inhassoro, Govuro, Vilanculos, Manica, Sofala and Palma-Afungi in various training programmes for organisations such as Sasol, TotalEnergies, CNE and Vulcan, amongst others.

LABOUR MARKET PREPARATION PROGRAMME (PPMT)

In this program, 1,000 people were trained in various vocational training courses such as: Industrial Maintenance Mechanics, Industrial Electricity, Welding, Heavy Machinery Operation, Hotel and Restaurant Management, Electricity Installation, Locksmithing, Auto Mechanics, Auto Electricity, Refrigeration.

The objective of this program, which was implemented from September 2021 to October 2023, aims to encourage the hiring of local labor, based on technical & behavioral training, mapping opportunities and creating a database of professionals residing in the areas of interference direct or indirect from the Moatize mine.

At the end of the program, 600 self-employment kits were distributed. This program was implemented in partnership with Vulcan Moçambique.

Training Programme in Cutting and Sewing and Women’s Entrepreneurship

One of Tecnicol's first projects in partnership with Vale Moçambique (recently Vulcan Moçambique) implemented in 2014. This project aimed to equip women in the District of Moatize with Cutting and Sewing skills so that they could make clothes and embroider.

In this program, 50 women were trained, who received work kits and created the association of Seamstresses of Moatize, which today is a Cooperative that continues to operate

Believe in Employment Programme (Programa Acredita Emprega)

The Believe in Employment program (Programa Acredita Emprega) was implemented in the south of Mozambique where more than 1,500 young people from the districts of KaMaxaquene, Manhiça, Matola and Matutuine were trained.

This program was implemented in partnership with SEJE and the World Bank and aims to improve access to education and employment opportunities for young people. Courses in Carpentry, Cooking, Cutting and Sewing, Refrigeration Systems, Auto Mechanics, Entrepreneurship, Hardware and Plumbing were taught

Let’s Do Business – E4D (Vamos empreender – E4D)

Entrepreneur development program with two components, namely, Entrepreneurship Training and Business Coaching. It was implemented in Inhassoro and Govuro in partnership with GIZ where 500 people were trained.

This program aimed to support local economic development in the districts of Inhassoro and Govuro, Inhambane Province in Mozambique, by offering demand-driven skills programs and supporting small and medium-sized enterprises (SMEs) to improve their overall capacity and, ultimately, improve rural development.

Financial Management Training and Small Business Promotion - FMT

The Financial Management Training and Small Business Promotion program was implemented in Afungi, Palma, in the province of Cabo Delgado in partnership with TotalEnergies. More than 400 people were trained in this program between 2019 and 2021.

The program aimed to provide resettled populations with financial management knowledge so that they can better manage their compensation.

In addition to training in financial management, Business Coaching, Mentoring sessions and the creation and establishment of savings groups were implemented.
